# ✅ SETUP GUIDE

## 🚀 YOUR APP IS READY!

After deploying, you'll get a URL like:
```
https://merrylight-erp.vercel.app/
```

## 📋 FIRST TIME SETUP (5 MIN)

### Step 1: Go to Master Files
- Click "Master Files" in sidebar

### Step 2: Add Customers
- Click "Customers" tab
- Click "+ Add"
- Enter customer names (e.g., ABC Exports, XYZ Foods)
- Click Save

### Step 3: Add Varieties
- Click "Varieties" tab
- Click "+ Add"
- Enter variety names (e.g., Arana, Bounty, Cascade)
- Click Save

### Step 4: Add Pack Types
- Click "Pack Types" tab
- Click "+ Add"
- Enter pack type names (e.g., Carton, Tray, Export Box)
- Click Save

## 🎯 THEN YOU CAN

1. **Fruit Receiving** - Upload fruit batches
2. **Packing Operations** - Create work orders
3. **Track everything** - In real-time

## 🎉 YOU'RE READY!

Your Merrylight ERP is fully functional!
